About Company


TKee- shirt is a leading national corporation which is located in Egypt, ElGouna. It was established in 2015 to induce a further spurt in clothes. Its aim is to produce air conditioned clothes which can adjust the temperature of the human body without even having an air conditioner. The idea was inspired by NASA. NASA produced suits for astronauts using outlast technology fabric that makes the body cool in hot climates and warm in cold climates using the concept of absorb, store and release heat. Therefore, a group of leading promising young businessmen established "Tkee-shirt" corporation to produce clothes which simulate those suits. The corporation made a partnership with the company Outlast Technologies LLC, USA which provides that material. TKee- shirt was able to import that material and design several types of clothes with an ambition to provide them for all the classes of people by trying to reduce the cost of the clothes. Although TKee- shirt has only been established since 2015, it has exported its products to many European, African and Asian countries. About 15% of the local market has been filled by TKee- shirt clothes because of its advantages and low budget. The company helps in rationalizing electricity as air conditioning systems are decreased.

The corporation consists of several groups; each group has certain tasks. The web group was able to create a website which was a principal factor in attracting many customers. Moreover, the application of the company has also provided several designs which appealed to customers. The Marketing team could design a leading marketing campaign all over the world. Our presentation team was able to do presentations which illustrated our performance to national and international companies. All the motivation which helped us in increasing our productivity was due to our creativity team's efforts. Currently, the company is constructing two other branches in Cairo and Alexandria to widen its productivity, boost the economy of Egypt and be a well- reputed company all over the world.
